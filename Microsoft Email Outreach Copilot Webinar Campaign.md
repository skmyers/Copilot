## What is this?
Cowork prompt to analyze all my MS Teams, SharePoint, Outlook, and OneDrive and identify accounts that fit specific criteria. Once identified, the prompt will create a draft in the Outlook Outbox using the email template in the prompt for you to review and click Send when ready.
